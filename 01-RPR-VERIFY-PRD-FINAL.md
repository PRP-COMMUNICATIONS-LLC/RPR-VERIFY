## PART 1: PRODUCT DEFINITION

### Product Vision
Build a market-leading solution for Australian false positive account freeze resolution by positioning ourselves as the regulatory authority on AUSTRAC interpretation and compliance system behavior analysis.

### What We Solve
**Customer Problem:** Legitimate customers get their bank accounts frozen due to false positive fraud alerts. Banks can't easily explain why. Resolution takes 45+ days.

**Root Cause (Our Understanding):** Global compliance systems sometimes misinterpret Australian-specific regulatory requirements, causing systematic false positives.

**Our Solution:** We identify the specific regulatory interpretation gap and help customers resolve their account freeze in 10 days.

### Target Customer
- **Primary:** Individuals with frozen bank accounts in Australia
- **Secondary:** Small businesses experiencing account freezes
- **Tertiary:** Banks/compliance teams (B2B compliance solution)

### Market Context
- Thousands of accounts frozen annually due to false positives
- 45-90 day standard resolution process (customer pain point)
- Global compliance systems lack Australian regulatory nuance
- Growing demand for compliance-specific expertise

## PART 2: FEATURE SPECIFICATION

### Core Feature Set (MVP)

#### Feature 1: Account Analysis
**What it does:** Analyzes customer's account activity and freeze timeline to identify the specific regulatory interpretation gap

**How it works:**
- Customer provides account freeze details
- System analyzes transaction patterns against AUSTRAC requirements
- Identifies systematic regulatory gap (NOT "vendor-specific system flaw")
- Prepares analysis for customer + bank

**Why it matters:**
- Gives customer understanding of why they were frozen
- Provides regulatory authority credibility
- Enables faster resolution with bank

**Regulatory Framing:**
- "We identify systematic regulatory interpretation gaps"
- NOT "We reverse-engineered your compliance system"
- NOT "We know which vendor system you use"

---

#### Feature 2: Regulatory Interpretation Guidance
**What it does:** Provides customer with AUSTRAC-based explanation of why the freeze occurred

**How it works:**
- Explains relevant AUSTRAC requirement

- Provides regulatory framework for resolution

**Why it matters:**
- Empowers customer with regulatory knowledge
- Differentiates from generic "appeal support"
- Positions us as regulatory authority

**Regulatory Framing:**
- "Our AUSTRAC expertise helps you understand the gap"
- NOT "Here's what the vendor system got wrong"

#### Feature 3: Bank Communication Support
**What it does:** Helps customer prepare communication with bank for account unfreezing

**How it works:**

- Explains AUSTRAC interpretation

- Provides documentation package

**Why it matters:**
- Speeds up bank resolution process
- Increases success rate (regulatory authority backing)

**Regulatory Framing:**

- Demonstrates reliability
